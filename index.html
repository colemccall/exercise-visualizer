How to get your Strava export
  1. Go to strava.com → Settings → My Account
  2. Scroll to "Download or Delete Your Account"
  3. Click "Request Your Archive" — you’ll get an email with a link
  4. Download the ZIP file and upload it here

Note: Strava exports most activities as .fit files (no map). Only activities with .gpx files will show on the heatmap.

How to export
  1. Open the Health app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your profile picture (top right)
  3. Tap "Export All Health Data"
  4. Share the resulting ZIP to your computer and drop it here

or drag & drop
How to export
  1. Go to connect.garmin.com
  2. Click your name → Account Settings → Data Management
  3. Under "Export Your Data" click "Request Data Export"
  4. Wait for an email with a download link, then drop the ZIP here
